Continuing your claim

To continue your claim online you will need to be able to:

  • upload receipts, or have a satisfactory reason for not providing evidence of your payment if you are claiming for:
    • train
    • coach
    • taxi
    • parking
    • plane
    • ferry
  • upload proof if you are claiming for:
    • overnight stays
    • lost income if self-employed
    • childcare
    • adult care

When you have all the information, you can continue with your claim.

If you unable to upload your proof, call 01924 205 200 or email witness.query@cps.gov.uk
