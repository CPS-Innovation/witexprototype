Start now

Before you start

You can claim expenses online if you:

  • are over 18
  • are claiming only for yourself

You must have the following for the day(s) you were in court:

  • your blue witness expense claim form
  • an email address which you can access
  • receipts or a satisfactory reason for not providing evidence of your
    payment for:
    • train
    • coach
    • taxi
    • parking
    • plane
    • ferry
  • proof if you are claiming for:
    • overnight stays
    • lost income if self-employed
    • childcare
    • adult care

You must be able to:

  • upload receipts as a PDF or an image
  • provide your bank account details if you want your expenses to be paid into a UK bank or building society, or non UK bank
  • provide a UK address if you want to be paid by cheque

We will deal with your claim as quickly as possible and, as long as you have filled the form in correctly, we aim to pay within 10 working days.

There’s a different process for claiming expenses in Scotland and claiming expenses in Northern Ireland.
