Overnight stay(s)

You can claim expenses for an overnight stay if:

  • it takes you 90 minutes or more to travel to court
  • you are disabled
  • you have another good reason for staying overnight

If you paid for your overnight stay(s) you can claim for your accommodation and a fixed amount to cover your meals and refreshments.

If you didn't pay for your stay(s), you can still claim a fixed allowance for food and drink.
