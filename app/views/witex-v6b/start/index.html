Claim witness expenses

Use this service to:

  • claim food, drink and travel expenses for being a witness in English and Welsh criminal courts
Start now

Before you start

You must:

  • have receipts if you are claiming for:
    • public transport
    • parking
    • other driving costs such as congestion charge or tolls
    • taxi
  • be able to upload receipts as a PDF or an image
  • have your sort code and account number if you want your expenses to be paid into a bank or building society

We will deal with your claim as quickly as possible and, as long as you have filled the form in correctly, we aim to pay within 10 working days.

The online service is also available in Welsh (ar gael yn Gymraeg).
