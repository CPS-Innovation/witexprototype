Before you start

You can claim expenses if you are a:

  • witness
  • parent or guardian of a witness under 18
  • person eligible to claim, for example you are helping a witness attend court
  • carer of an adult witness

If you're helping someone else, answer the questions as if you are them.

You must be able to:

  • upload receipts as a PDF or an image
  • provide your bank account details if you want your expenses to be paid into a UK bank or building society, or non UK bank
  • provide a UK address if you want to be paid by cheque

We will deal with your claim as quickly as possible and, as long as you have filled the form in correctly, we aim to pay within 10 working days.

There’s a different process for claiming expenses in Scotland and claiming expenses in Northern Ireland.
