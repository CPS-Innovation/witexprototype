Claiming for your travel

You can claim travel costs to and from court.

You need to show receipts for:

  • train
  • coach
  • parking
  • taxi

You won't need to show receipts for:

  • London transport (including tube, bus, Docklands Light Railway (DLR), Elizabeth line, overground)
  • bus/metro/tram outside London
  • other driving costs such as congestion charge or tolls

You can claim a fixed amount per mile for:

  • car
  • motorbike
  • bicycle
