{% include "includes/cps/whatDay.html" %}Claiming for your travel

You can claim travel costs to and from court.

! Warning You could be prosecuted if you claim for costs already paid for by the Crown Prosecution Service (CPS) or the court.

You need to show receipts or a satisfactory reason for not providing evidence of your payment for:

  • train
  • coach
  • parking
  • taxi
  • plane
  • ferry

You won't need to show receipts for:

  • Public transport within London (Zone 1 -9) (including tube, bus, river bus, Docklands Light Railway (DLR), London Trams, Elizabeth Line and overground train)
  • bus, metro, or tram outside London
  • other driving costs such as Congestion Charge or tolls

You can claim a fixed amount per mile for:

  • car
  • motorbike
  • bicycle
