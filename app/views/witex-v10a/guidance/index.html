Before you start

You can claim expenses online if you:

  • are over 18
  • are claiming only for yourself
  • claim for one day at a time
  • have not had travel costs paid for in advance by the Crown Prosecution Service (CPS), the police or the court
  • did not travel to court by air or sea

You must have :

  • your blue witness expense claim form
  • an email address which you can access
  • receipts or a satisfactory reason for not providing evidence of your payment:
    • train
    • coach
    • taxi
    • parking
  • receipts if you are claiming for:
    • overnight stays
    • lost income for self-employed
    • childcare
    • adult care
    • covid tests
  • You must have proof of self employment if you are claiming for:
    • lost income for self employed
  • be able to upload receipts as a PDF or an image
  • have your sort code and account number if you want your expenses to be paid into a bank or building society

We will deal with your claim as quickly as possible and, as long as you have filled the form in correctly, we aim to pay within 10 working days.

There’s a different process for claiming expenses in Scotland and claiming expenses in Northern Ireland.
