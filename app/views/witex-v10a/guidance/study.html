Travel and parking costs

You can claim for different methods of travelling to and from court.

You need to show receipts or a satisfactory reason for not providing evidence of your payment for:

  • train
  • coach
  • parking
  • taxi

You won't need to show receipts for:

  • London transport (including tube, bus, Docklands Light Railway (DLR), Elizabeth line, overground train)
  • bus/metro/tram outside London
  • other driving costs such as Congestion Charge or tolls

You can claim a fixed amount per mile for:

  • car
  • motorbike
  • bicycle
